Hi {first_name},

Here's what we've been working on this month to make Driiva faster, smoother, and more reliable.

---

### Faster sign-in

We fixed a delay that was making sign-in take up to 20 seconds for some users. Logging in should now feel instant. If you've ever seen a spinning screen after entering your password, that's gone.

### Smoother onboarding

The setup flow when you first join Driiva has been rebuilt from the ground up. It works the same — same steps, same questions — but it's faster and less likely to glitch on older phones.

### Notifications are live

The bell icon on your dashboard now actually works. Tap it to see your notifications. Right now it'll say "No new notifications" — but this is the foundation for trip scores, refund updates, and achievement alerts coming soon.

### Better trip recording

We've stress-tested the trip recording screen with dozens of scenarios: pausing mid-drive, losing GPS signal, going offline, and cancelling. All of these now handle gracefully instead of showing errors.

### Leaderboard loads faster

The weekly leaderboard now caches results so it loads instantly instead of hitting the database every time. Your rank still updates in real time when you complete a trip.

### Mobile feel

- Pull down to refresh your dashboard
- Subtle haptic feedback when you tap buttons (on supported devices)
- Smoother loading animations — no more blank white screens
- Swipeable trip cards on the trips page
- Score, miles, and refund numbers now animate when they change

### Behind the scenes

- 299 automated tests now run on every code change (up from 197)
- Security audit completed — 12 issues found and fixed
- Error monitoring live across all backend services
- Production deployment pipeline with manual approval gate

---

### Coming next

- Push notifications for trip scores and refund updates
- Stripe checkout for premium payments
- Community pool calculations going live
- Rewards programme (Tesco, Halfords, Nectar) eligibility based on your score
